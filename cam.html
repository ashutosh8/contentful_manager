Tips - Points to Remember

  • It is advisable to migrate maximum of 4 to 5 assets at a time due to Contentful API limitation of 10 requests per second.
  • Migrating multiple locales of an asset together is not recommended. That may cause version conflict.
  • One needs to publish the assets manually after migration from one space to another.
